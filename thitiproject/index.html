Watford 0-3 Arsenal

This post is written by Thiti

Arsenal produced a superb second-half display to defeat Watford and move into second place in the Premier League. Alexis Sanchez opened the scoring after the break when he pounced on the loose ball after Mesut Ozil appeared to be fouled and fired in.

Olivier Giroud slammed in the second, before Aaron Ramsey slotted in his first for the club this season. Watford's best chance came in the first half when Odion Ighalo screwed his shot wide from 12 yards.

Arsenal 3-0 Manchester United

This post is written by Thiti

Arsenal stunned Manchester United with three goals in the opening 20 minutes as the Gunners moved up to second in the Premier League table.

United had no answer to Arsenal's opening burst as Alexis Sanchez and Mesut Ozil scored twice in 74 seconds. Sanchez added a third from 18 yards as the Gunners beat United for just the second time in 14 league meetings.
